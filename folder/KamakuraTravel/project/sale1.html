Omamori are Japanese amulets commonly sold at Shinto shrines and Buddhist temples, dedicated to particular Shinto kami as well as Buddhist figures, and are said to provide various forms of luck or protection. This Kamakura Shirasaki Hachiman Shrine omamori provides luck such as peach blossom, happiness in love, marriage, academic progress, passing tests, and obtaining qualifications.
